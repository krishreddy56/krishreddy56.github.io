Motivation

How does LendingTree make money ?

Lenders pay LendingTree for the chance to compete for the borrower's business.

LendingTree passes your profile to up to five lenders who then provide borrower a customized loan offer based on borrower's request.


To dig deep in their business model, we can divide the business in two parts:


  1. Short term business model

    2. The number of users putting up their data to get loan offers.

    Per user data price LendingTree charges to the lenders.


  2. Long term Business Model

    3. To really keep the business going in the long run, it is important that LendingTree helps the borrowers to get a good loan offers so that they keep getting enough users.

    With that, LendingTree also requires that the lenders gain a reasonable profit in order to get more number of competing Lenders and keep the price of data per user such that LendingTree maximises on the profit.


What do we do about it ?

Taking these two Business models, we try to understand the data by creating an interactive statewise visualisation by keeping Loan Status as the major point of evaluation with Amortization, Credit Score, Property type, Lender ID.
